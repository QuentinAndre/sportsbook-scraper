# How to Export Your Bet History

This guide walks you through exporting your betting history using the Sportsbook History Exporter Chrome extension.

## Is This Extension Safe?

Yes. This extension only reads the bet history that is already visible on your screen when you click "Start Scraping". It does not access your password, your account details, your balance, or any other personal information. It cannot read or modify any other website. Everything happens on your computer — no data is sent anywhere online. You can verify this yourself by reviewing the extension's permissions on the Chrome Web Store page, and you are welcome to remove the extension after completing the survey.

## Requirements

- Google Chrome browser (desktop only)
- A sportsbook account with betting history

## Step 1: Install the Extension

1. Open Google Chrome on your computer.
2. Visit the Chrome Web Store page for the extension: [LINK TO BE PROVIDED BY QUENTIN]
3. Click "Add to Chrome".
4. When prompted, click "Add extension" to confirm.
5. You should see a small funnel icon appear in your Chrome toolbar (top-right area). If you don't see it, click the puzzle piece icon in the toolbar and pin "Sportsbook History Exporter".

## Step 2: Navigate to Your Bet History

Navigate to your sportsbook's settled bets page using the Google Chrome browser, as indicated in the survey instructions.

## Step 3: Export Your Bets

1. Once you are on your settled bets history page, click the funnel icon in your Chrome toolbar. A small popup will appear.
2. Click the "Start Scraping" button.
3. The extension will automatically scroll through your bet history to load all your bets. DO NOT NAVIGATE AWAY OR CLICK ANYWHERE DURING THIS STEP.
4. When scrolling is complete, the extension will generate a CSV file and your browser will prompt you to save it.
5. Save the file to a location you can easily find (e.g., your Desktop or Downloads folder). The file will be named something like "bets_2026-02-26T09-45-00.csv".

## Step 4: Upload the File

1. Return to the survey.
2. In the file upload question, click "Choose File" or "Browse".
3. Select the CSV file you just saved.
4. Confirm the upload.

## Troubleshooting

- "No bets found on this page." Make sure you are on the correct page ("My Bets" > "Settled" tab). The extension only works on this specific page.

- The extension icon is grayed out or missing. Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome's toolbar, find "Sportsbook History Exporter" in the list, and click the pin icon to make it visible.

- The file did not download. Check that Chrome is not blocking downloads. You may see a download bar at the bottom of your browser window or a prompt asking where to save the file.

- The extension does not work on my phone. The extension is only available on the desktop version of Google Chrome. It does not work on mobile browsers.

## Removing the Extension

Once you have completed the survey, you can remove the extension from your browser:

1. Right-click the funnel icon in your Chrome toolbar.
2. Select "Remove from Chrome".
3. Click "Remove" to confirm.

Alternatively, you can go to chrome://extensions in your address bar, find "Sportsbook History Exporter", and click "Remove".
