Privacy Policy

Sportsbook Bet History Exporter — Last updated February 26, 2026

Overview

Sportsbook Bet History Exporter is a Chrome extension that helps you export your own bet history from FanDuel and DraftKings as a CSV file. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

Data collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to any external server, analytics service, or third party.
  • No cookies, tokens, or login credentials are read or stored.
  • No browsing history or activity is tracked.
  • The extension does not use any remote code.

What the extension accesses

When you click "Start Scraping" on a supported sportsbook page, the extension reads bet information (dates, odds, stakes, results) from the page you are currently viewing. This data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device, within your browser.
  • Converted to CSV format in memory.
  • Saved to your local machine via Chrome's Downloads API only when you initiate the export.

No data ever leaves your computer.

Host permissions

The extension requests access to *.fanduel.com and *.draftkings.com solely to read bet history content from those pages. These permissions are not used for any other purpose.
